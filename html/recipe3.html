Healthy Hummus

Recipe by: Wholefoodscollective.com.au

Ingredients

  • 1 tin chickpeas (approx 250g rinsed)
  • 2 tbsp tahini (hulled)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (juice of)
  • 1/2 to 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin

Directions

  1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, oil, lemon, garlic, salt, and cumin.
  2. Add water to reach desired thickness.
  3. Top with olive oil, seeds, and fresh basil.
