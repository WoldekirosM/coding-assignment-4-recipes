Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Pour into waffle maker.
  3. Cook until golden.
